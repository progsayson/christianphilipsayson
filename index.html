About Me

I am Christian Philip S. Sayson, a graduate of the Bachelor of Industrial Technology — Major in Computer Technology at Cebu Technological University — San Francisco Campus, Batch 2026.

Graduated with Cum Laude distinction — an honor earned through consistent dedication, a love for learning, and the guidance of exceptional mentors throughout the program.

I have practical experience in web development using HTML, CSS, and Java, along with server-side tools such as XAMPP, phpMyAdmin, MySQL, and MariaDB. I am also skilled in Linux system administration, including the installation and configuration of Ubuntu servers, Drupal CMS, and database environments.

On the hardware side, I have hands-on experience in IoT and embedded systems — specifically integrating ESP8266 Wi-Fi modules with Arduino microcontrollers and RFID scanners for smart security applications. I also proudly served as a Research Leader, spearheading a capstone study on enhancing library catalog accessibility through web-based feedback systems.
